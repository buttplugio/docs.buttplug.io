# Foreo

## Overview

Foreo produces Bluetooth LE facial cleansing and wellness devices. The Luna Fofo includes skin conductivity sensing in addition to a vibration motor, and reports battery level over BLE.

## Devices

| Device | BLE Name | Protocol Page | Features |
|--------|----------|---------------|----------|
| Luna Fofo | FOFO | [Protocol](foreo.md) | Vibration, battery, skin conductivity |
