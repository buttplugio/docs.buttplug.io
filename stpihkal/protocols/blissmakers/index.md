# Blissmakers

## Overview

Blissmakers makes BLE-connected vibrators available under multiple device names.

## Devices

| Device | BLE Name | Protocol Page | Features |
|--------|----------|---------------|----------|
| Blissmakers (various) | AR-085-M-C2, AR-JX052X, AR-073-M-D2, and others | [Protocol](blissmakers.md) | Vibration (0–10), stop |
