# MonsterPub

## Overview

MonsterPub is a brand by Sistalk that produces Bluetooth LE egg vibrators. These devices are LiBo sub-brand products and use the LiBo vibration protocol. Newer Gen 2 devices require an additional authentication handshake.

See the [LiBo brand page](../libo/index.md) and [LiBo vibration protocol](../libo/libo-vibes.md) for full protocol details.

| MonsterPub (Gen 2, with pressure sensors) | MonsterPub | [LiBo Vibes Protocol](../libo/libo-vibes.md) | Vibration, patterns, pressure sensing |
