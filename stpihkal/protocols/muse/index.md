# Mu Se / Love Spouse

## Overview

Mu Se (also known as Love Spouse) is an OEM platform used by multiple brands including Dream Lover and Galaku for panty vibrators and similar wearables, controlled over BLE with a simple 2-byte command protocol.

## Devices

| Device | BLE Name | Protocol Page | Features |
|--------|----------|---------------|----------|
| Dream Lover Archer 2 (WB-ZDB-WST) | varies | [Mu Se](muse.md) | Vibration speed, patterns |
| Galaku Panty Vib (WB-TDD) | varies | [Mu Se](muse.md) | Vibration speed, patterns |
