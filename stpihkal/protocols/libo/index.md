# LiBo

## Overview

LiBo (also sold under the Sistalk brand) produces Bluetooth LE vibrators, shock eggs, suction devices, and inflating vibrators. Many devices from other brands (including Sistalk MonsterPub) are LiBo rebrands. Most devices share a common `0x6000` service for vibration control, with per-device exceptions for shock, suction, and inflation.
