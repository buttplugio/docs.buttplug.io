# Lelo

## Overview

Lelo is a Swedish manufacturer of premium intimate devices. They produce several lines of BLE-connected products including the F1s series and the Ida Wave.

## Common BLE Patterns

- Service UUID: `0000fff0-0000-1000-8000-00805f9b34fb` (shared across some models)
- Some devices (F1s v2, Ida Wave) use a pairing/security characteristic at `00000a11-...`
