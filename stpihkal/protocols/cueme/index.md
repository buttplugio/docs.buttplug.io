# Cueme

## Overview

Cueme produces Bluetooth LE vibrating underwear in three form factors: men's underwear, bra, and women's underwear. All variants share the same service layout. Device advertisement names encode the MAC address and form factor.

## Devices

| Device | BLE Name | Protocol Page | Features |
|--------|----------|---------------|----------|
| Men's Underwear | FUNCODE\_\<mac\>\_1 | [Protocol](cueme.md) | Multi-motor vibration |
| Bra | FUNCODE\_\<mac\>\_2 | [Protocol](cueme.md) | Multi-motor vibration |
| Women's Underwear | FUNCODE\_\<mac\>\_3 | [Protocol](cueme.md) | Multi-motor vibration |
