# Hisionlee / Totenjoy Rabbit Protocol

## Introduction

The Hisionlee/Totenjoy Rabbit is a BLE-connected vibrator. Protocol details have not yet been reverse-engineered and documented.

## Notes

- Device is owned by at least one contributor but reverse engineering has not been completed.
- BLE advertisement name, service UUIDs, and command format are all unknown.
