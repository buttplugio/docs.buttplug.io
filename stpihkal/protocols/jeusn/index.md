# JEUSN

## Overview

JEUSN makes BLE-connected vibrators with oscillation and heating functions.

|--------|----------|---------------|----------|
| JEUSN Lush (various) | JEU-Lush13F-XT(BLE), JEU-Lush13E-XT | [Protocol](jeusn.md) | Vibration, oscillation, heater |
