# Satisfyer

## Overview

Satisfyer is a major manufacturer of BLE-controlled intimate devices. They produce a large range of products that all share the same BLE protocol using custom 128-bit service UUIDs. The device-config lists dozens of device variants.

## Common BLE Patterns

- Service UUID: `51361500-c5e7-47c7-8a6e-47ebc99d80e8` ("live control")
- Devices do not broadcast a name in BLE advertisements; the name is only available after the first GATT connection.
