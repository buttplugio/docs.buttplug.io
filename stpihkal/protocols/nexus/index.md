# Nexus

## Overview

Nexus produces prostate massagers and vibrators with BLE control. The Revo line features independent vibration and rotation control with multiple speed and direction options.

## Devices

| Device | BLE Name | Protocol Page | Features |
|--------|----------|---------------|----------|
| Nexus Revo | XW-LW3 | [Nexus Revo](nexus-revo.md) | Vibration, rotation (CW/CCW), battery |
